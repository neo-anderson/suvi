Suvi confirms working on the new album

Suvi has confirmed what we all suspected. Earlier today she tweeted a picture from a studio describing it #SV. This is the hashtag fans use to describe Suvi's yet untitled 8th studio album. We've already seen images of Suvi outside recording studios, joined by other musicians (including by Kanye West and Coldplay's Chris Martin). So far we've only heard a confirmation from DreamWorks Animation that she is working on a concept album for their animated movie "Home", which will be out early next year! Can't wait!
